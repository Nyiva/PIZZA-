Que Pasa is a busy, friendly, neighbourhood bar and restaurant offering locals and visitors alike an informal place to eat, drink, lounge and relax. It aims to create a stylish yet simple space with sliding doors that open onto the street, vast wooden chandeliers hanging from high ceilings, bare stone walls, tiled floors, and an upstairs lounge area overlooking the new dining area. Wood-fired pizzas are the cornerstone of Que Pasa’s menu, with hand-made dough being baked from early morning till late at night.Their continental menu ranges from steak and fish dishes to gourmet pasta bowls all served up on candle lit wooden tables.
